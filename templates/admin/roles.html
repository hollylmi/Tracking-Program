How it works
  • Add each position (e.g. Supervisor, Operator, Labourer)
  • Set the delay rate for each — the rate charged to the client per hour during a delay
  • When adding an employee, select their role and the rate auto-fills
  • You can override the rate per employee if needed
  • Delay reports group by role title (not individual names)
